While therapy often focuses on healing past wounds and treating mental health conditions, coaching is future-oriented and action-focused. Coaching helps you set and achieve goals, develop skills, and maximize your potential. We don't diagnose or treat mental health conditions but can work in conjunction with therapists when needed.

The duration varies based on your goals and commitment level. Most clients see meaningful progress within 3-6 months with weekly sessions. Some clients continue coaching for a year or more to achieve more complex goals or maintain accountability. We recommend starting with a 3-month commitment for optimal results.

Results vary by individual, but common outcomes include increased clarity about goals and priorities, improved confidence and decision-making, better work-life balance, career advancement, stronger relationships, and greater overall life satisfaction.
